Paper SPAV: Spanish Through Audio-Visual Media

The papers are from Section B of Paper SPB3 of the Modern And Medieval Languages Tripos Part IB.

Instructions

The Spanish Through Audio-Visual Media test carries 40% of the total marks for paper SPB3.

The examination lasts for NINETY MINUTES, starting from the beginning of the first play-through of the tape. In examination conditions the test passage is played TWICE. You may take notes during the playing of the test passage, but you may NOT look at the questions until after the first playing. FIVE minutes are then allowed. The passage is played a second time, after which the invigilator will tell you the exact time at which the exam will end and you may then begin to write your answers.

Each paper consists of a video clip and transcript, and the original question paper. You can use the papers as practise examinations following the steps of the listening comprehension exam including any time limits, or just use them for listening comprehension practise.

If you wish to use this as a specimen examination, first listen to the extract and make notes if you wish. If you take notes, try and record the main gist of what has been said, together with any key information. Write your notes in Spanish, otherwise you will waste time unnecessarily in translation.

Then look at the questions for five minutes. Fill in as much as you can of the answers and bear in mind the questions which have been asked in preparation for your second hearing of the extract.

Then listen to the extract again, making notes accordingly. Do not at this point attempt to write full sentences; just record the essential information.

Write up your answers. You have quite a long time to do this in the exam (the remainder of the hour). Write full sentences, but do not elaborate unnecessarily on your answers; try and be succinct but accurate. Do not be afraid of using words and phrases from the extract itself: you are not required to paraphrase the extract. Check your Spanish: 25% of the marks are given for accuracy in Spanish usage irrespective of the content of your answers.
